Our Product

The core product of our brand is the “authentic bag.” These handbags are made of 100% high-quality materials such as wet blue-goat leather with printed grain, salpa, silk organza & nickel, gold, and antique brass. The most interesting aspect of this product is its unique design. “Authentic bags” are designed as silhouettes of the human body or body parts with imperfections as decorations which makes this bag interesting and unique. This way we want to convey the main idea of our concept that imperfections are gifts to be worked with, not shames to be hidden.
Mission: embracing the unique features of the body, underline the imperfections
Type of Fashion product: handbag
Color Palette: different skin/nude tones
Design: asymmetric; the shape of body parts with imperfections represented other decorative materials
